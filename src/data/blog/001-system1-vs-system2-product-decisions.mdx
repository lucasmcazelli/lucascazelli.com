## <span id="system-1">System 1: Fast and Intuitive</span>
Quick heuristics drive velocity. Useful for small UX tweaks and low-risk calls.

## <span id="system-2">System 2: Slow and Deliberate</span>
Structured analysis drives confidence. Use for pricing, architecture and bets.

## <span id="how-to-apply">How to Apply in Product</span>
- Start with System 1 to generate options
- Switch to System 2 for irreversible or high-impact choices
- Instrument decisions and learn
